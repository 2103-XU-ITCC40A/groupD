Online Admissions & Enrolment Process for Takers and Passers of the Online College Entrance Examination

Higher Education Online Admissions and Enrolment Procedure for 1st Semester SY 2022-2023
For TAKERS and PASSERS of the XU Entrance Examinations




A. ADMISSIONS & ENROLMENT


----Step 1.-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Register for and take the Online College Entrance Examination (OCEE). Details of the registration process and examination dates are found on this
page: https://www.xu.edu.ph/testing-dates.

An entrance exam is required for the following undergraduate programs: Accountancy, Nursing, Engineering (except Industrial Engineering),
Education, and Psychology. Click here to view the comprehensive programs list.

----Step 2.-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Go to this link apply.xu.edu.ph/exam_results to view the test results. If score meets the general cutoff, or if eligible for admission to a certain
program, then applicant creates an account in the admissions portal.

After successful processing of application in the admissions portal, an ADMISSIONS CLEARANCE will be sent to the applicant’s registered email. The
Admissions Clearance will be used to proceed with the online enrolment registration. here to view the comprehensive programs list.

----Step 3.-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Pay the required down payment of Php3,000.00
Go to this link https://www.xu.edu.ph/payment-centers-finance to view the list of accredited XU payment centers.

IMPORTANT: Secure ADMISSIONS CLEARANCE prior to making a down payment. Carefully follow the instructions in filling out the bank forms as
indicated on the Admissions Clearance.

----Step 4.-----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------

Process Online Enrolment Registration for school/college by accessing this link
https://www.xu.edu.ph/admissions-enrollment

Prepare clear photos or scanned copies of the following documents:

a) Admissions Clearance
b) Grade 12 Report Card (Form 138) for Freshmen/ Transfer Credentials (formerly called Honorable Dismissal) for Transferees
c) PSA Birth Certificate
d) Validated Down Payment Deposit Slip (or Notice of Scholarship Award for scholars)
e) Signed Data Privacy Consent Form (downloadable from the enrolment registration form)
After completing the online registration, a notification will be emailed to the applicant regarding the status of their application for enrolment in XU. If
successful, they will be given their Login credentials to access the Student Lifecycle Management and Information System (SLMIS) to view their class
schedule and other related information.
